01About

I live in the first mile of a product — where it's still an idea on a whiteboard and nobody's sure it can be built.

I'm Chris — a founder, product builder, and writer. I start companies and products before there's a roadmap, a deck, or proof the thing can exist. Hand me a blank page and a hard problem, and I'll hand you back a shipped first version.

I build at the edge of human biology and technology — longevity, the nervous system, the quiet signals our bodies send that we've never been able to read. I turn those signals into products people can hold, and I obsess over how something feels as much as how it scales.

My whole game is compressing the distance between vision and reality: frame the ambiguous, ship 0 → 1, find the real signal, then build the small, sharp team — and the company around it — that takes a prototype to the world.
